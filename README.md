# Hack-Xol
Small form factor toolhead originally designed to fit the Sovol Zero
This toolhead takes inspiration from multiple toolheads, Micro Saul2, Xol Toolhead and Dragonburner.

Current supported hotends
Rapido/Dragon and any hotend that shares their mounting pattern

The extruder mount for my nebula is taken from the Dragonburner and their LGX lite mount and it fits direct. All thought untested, I believe those mounts for other extruders may fit as well.

Additonally, I am by no means an expert in fusion/design, but I'm always open to feedback on how to improve!

BOM,
M3 and M2 Screws
M3 and M2 heatsets
